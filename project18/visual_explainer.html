Understanding Monte Carlo Simulation for Stranded Assets

Simulating 10,000 possible futures to quantify climate transition risk.

What Is Monte Carlo Simulation?

Instead of predicting ONE future, we simulate thousands of possible futures, each with random shocks. This gives us a distribution of outcomes — not just a point estimate.
